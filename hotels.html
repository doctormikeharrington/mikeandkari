Hotels

Where to stay in Winnipeg

We've gathered some accommodation options near our wedding venues in downtown Winnipeg. We recommend booking early, especially if you're visiting from out of town.

The Fort Garry Hotel

222 Broadway, Winnipeg

Our reception venue! Stay where the party is and enjoy the convenience of being on-site. This grand heritage hotel offers luxurious rooms and excellent service.

To Ceremony: ~1 km (5 min drive)
To Reception: On-site

Visit Website

Norwood Hotel

112 Marion Street, Winnipeg

Located in Winnipeg's historic St. Boniface neighbourhood, just across the river from downtown. Features newly renovated rooms with walk-in rain showers, complimentary WiFi, and free parking. On-site restaurant and tavern available.

To Ceremony: ~2.5 km (7 min drive)
To Reception: ~2 km (6 min drive)

Visit Website

Fairmont Winnipeg

2 Lombard Place, Winnipeg

A premier downtown hotel connected to Winnipeg Square shopping. Modern amenities with easy access to both venues.

To Ceremony: ~1.5 km (5 min drive)
To Reception: ~1 km (4 min drive)

Visit Website

Delta Hotels Winnipeg

350 St Mary Ave, Winnipeg

A comfortable downtown option with modern rooms and great amenities. Walking distance to both wedding venues.

To Ceremony: ~1.2 km (4 min drive)
To Reception: ~800 m (3 min drive)

Visit Website

Inn at the Forks

75 Forks Market Rd, Winnipeg

Located at The Forks, one of Winnipeg's most popular destinations. Boutique hotel with a spa and excellent dining.

To Ceremony: ~2 km (6 min drive)
To Reception: ~1.5 km (5 min drive)

Visit Website

Booking Tips

August is a popular time to visit Winnipeg, so we recommend booking your accommodation as early as possible. Downtown hotels will give you the easiest access to both venues.

If you're looking for more budget-friendly options, there are also several hotels along Pembina Highway with good transit access to downtown.
