Where to find us on the big day

Ceremony

3:30 PM

All Saints Anglican Church

520 Broadway, Winnipeg, MB R3C 0W2

A beautiful historic church in the heart of downtown Winnipeg. Please arrive by 3:15 PM to be seated before the ceremony begins.

Get Directions

Parking Availability

Parking is available in the Canada Life lot, south of Broadway between Colony and Balmoral. Please do not park in any spots that are marked "Reserved 24 hours".

A small number of accessible parking spots are available at the church for those with mobility issues. There is an accessible entrance to the church in the courtyard between the church and West Broadway Commons (see map) and an accessible, barrier-free, universal toilet room in the adjoining Annex.

Cocktails & Reception

5:00 PM – Late

The Fort Garry Hotel

222 Broadway, Winnipeg, MB R3C 0R3

One of Winnipeg's most iconic heritage hotels, The Fort Garry Hotel offers timeless elegance and classic charm. The hotel is just a 5-minute drive from the church.

Parking: Valet parking is available at the hotel, as well as a parking garage across the street.

Get Directions

Getting Between Venues

The Fort Garry Hotel is approximately 1 km (0.6 miles) from All Saints Anglican Church—about a 5-minute drive or 12-minute walk along Broadway.

After the ceremony, you'll have time to make your way to the hotel before cocktail hour begins at 5:00 PM.
